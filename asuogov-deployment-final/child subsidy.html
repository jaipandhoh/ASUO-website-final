Childcare Subsidy Program

Supporting UO student parents with childcare assistance

Learn about 2025–26 program changes

About the Childcare Subsidy

Helps UO student parents by reimbursing 50% of eligible childcare costs up to an age-based maximum. Apply, be admitted, then submit monthly claims.

Funded by ASUO Incidental Fees and administered by the Basic Needs Program.

Program Eligibility

  • Enrolled in ≥1 credit (current or upcoming quarter).
  • Charged the Incidental Mandatory Fee for the term when care occurred (verify in DuckWeb).

Application Process

Apply on Engage (rolling: July 8, 2025–June 30, 2026, if funding allows).

  • Apply annually; review order: first-come.
  • Decision within ~10 working days.
  • Earliest reimbursable services: first day of the month you applied (no prior months).
  • Waitlist may apply if capacity is reached.
  • Program year: July 1–June 30.

Eligible Care Providers

  • Either: registered/licensed in the state of care or pass a background check approved by program staff.
  • Oregon "registered/certified" includes: licensed centers; registered/certified family homes; ERDC providers; regulated subsidy providers; recorded programs.
  • Verify Oregon providers: findchildcareoregon.org • Categories: Child Care Licensing Division
  • Unregistered providers: submit background-check documentation (identity, agency, dates/expiry, pass status). Common option: CJIS (Oregon State Police).
  • Costs for background checks are the participant's responsibility; Care-Finding Fund may help.
  • Only childcare costs tied to UO academics/work are reimbursable; K–12 private-school tuition excluded if a public alternative exists.
  • Participants/spouses/partners cannot be reimbursed as providers.

Claims Process

Submit Monthly Reimbursement Claim

Documentation

  • Invoice/receipt with provider identifiers, payer name, date, total, and provider contact; otherwise use the Alternative Receipt Documentation Form (hand signatures allowed digitally).

Participant Responsibilities

  • Submit by the 24th for that month's processing.
  • Claims batched month-end; refunds typically early next month.
  • One claim per month; include up to 31 days of care.
  • File within 60 days of services (email if extenuating circumstances).

Outcomes

  • Approved: refund via direct deposit (if set up) or US mail; first claim may take longer for account setup.
  • Denied: missing/insufficient documentation.

Details

  • Deposits/pre-payments reimbursed in the month care is provided.
  • No reimbursement for services before the application month start.
  • Co-applicants: primary participant submits the claim.
