Work with SGES

Join our team and support ASUO officers and recognized organizations

With ~25 student employees, SGES delivers services in seven key areas that support ASUO officers and recognized organizations.

Key Service Areas

  • Operations & continuity - Ensuring smooth day-to-day operations
  • Org services & advising - Supporting student organizations
  • Events & programming support - Helping create memorable campus events
  • Marketing & creative - Promoting ASUO initiatives
  • Financial support - Managing budgets and funding
  • Training & education - Developing student leaders
  • Engagement & success initiatives - Building campus community
