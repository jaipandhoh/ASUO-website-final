Resources for Graduate Students

Specialized resources and opportunities for graduate students at the University of Oregon through ASUO.

üéì Graduate Student Representation

Graduate students have unique needs and perspectives that ASUO works to address through dedicated representation and services:

Graduate Student Senate Seats

  • Dedicated Representation: Graduate students have specific Senate seats representing their interests
  • Policy Advocacy: Graduate student senators advocate for policies affecting graduate education
  • Funding Priorities: Input on how I-Fee funds are allocated to graduate student services
  • Academic Affairs: Representation on academic committees and policy discussions

Graduate Student Organizations

  • Graduate Student Union: Connect with the Graduate Student Union for additional advocacy
  • Departmental Groups: ASUO supports graduate student organizations within departments
  • Interdisciplinary Networks: Connect with graduate students across different programs

ASUO provides various financial resources specifically designed for graduate students:

Research and Conference Funding

  • Conference Travel: Apply for funding to present research at academic conferences
  • Research Materials: Funding for research supplies and equipment
  • Publication Costs: Support for academic publishing and open access fees
  • Professional Development: Workshops, training, and professional development opportunities

Emergency and Support Funding

  • Emergency Grants: Financial assistance for unexpected expenses
  • Childcare Support: Additional childcare funding for graduate student parents
  • Housing Assistance: Resources for housing-related financial challenges
  • Healthcare Support: Information about graduate student health insurance options

ASUO supports graduate students' academic and professional growth through various programs:
