Resources for Doctoral Students

Specialized resources and opportunities for doctoral students at the University of Oregon through ASUO.

Doctoral students have unique needs and perspectives that ASUO works to address through dedicated representation and specialized services:

Doctoral Student Senate Seats

  • Dedicated Representation: Doctoral students have specific Senate seats representing their interests
  • Research Advocacy: Doctoral student senators advocate for research funding and support
  • Academic Policy: Input on policies affecting doctoral education and research
  • Graduate Student Union: Collaboration with the Graduate Student Union for advocacy

Research and Academic Support

  • Research Funding: Information about ASUO funding for doctoral research
  • Conference Support: Funding for conference presentations and research dissemination
  • Publication Support: Resources for academic publishing and open access
  • Research Ethics: Information about research ethics and compliance

ASUO provides various financial resources specifically designed for doctoral students:

  • Conference Travel: Apply for funding to present research at academic conferences
  • Research Materials: Funding for research supplies, equipment, and software
  • Publication Costs: Support for academic publishing and open access fees
  • Professional Development: Workshops, training, and professional development opportunities

Emergency and Support Funding

  • Emergency Grants: Financial assistance for unexpected expenses
  • Childcare Support: Additional childcare funding for doctoral student parents
  • Housing Assistance: Resources for housing-related financial challenges
  • Healthcare Support: Information about doctoral student health insurance options

ASUO supports doctoral students' academic and professional growth through various programs:
